Depression Indicator

Take a 3 Minute Test

This depression quiz is based on the Depression Screening Test developed by Ivan K. Goldberg, MD, the founder of Psycom who was also a renowned psychiatrist.

Instructions: Below are some list of questions that relate to life experiences common among people who have been diagnosed with depression. Please read each question carefully, and indicate how often you have experienced the same or similar challenges in the past 2 weeks.


Do you lack interest or pleasure in doing things?


Do you feel hopeless, depressed or down?


Do you have trouble falling or staying asleep, or do you sleep excessively?


Do you often lack energy or feel tired?


Do you often have experiences with poor appetite, weight loss or overeating?


Do you often feel bad about yourself - etc. that you are a failure or have let yourself or your family down?


Do you have trouble concentrating on daily activities? - etc while reading newspapers or watching television.


Do you feel slow or moody while talking with others?


You think that you would be better off dead, or would feel like hurting yourself?


If you've had any experiences with issues above, how difficult would these problems make your life be at work, home, school, or with other people?
